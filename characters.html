Characters

Red Monkey

Grandson of Red George (the Red Manor King), heir to the Red Manor, and The Manor Factory. A somewhat shy and moody monkey, but his heart is in the right place.

Green Heart

Red Monkey's oldest friend. He's heart shaped, and extremely lovable, although a bit quirky. There's a mystery concerning his origin.

Rufles

The Red Manor's trusty buttler. He took care of Red Monkey and Green Heart while growing up, he's been there since forever...?
